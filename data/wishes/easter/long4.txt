Christ is risen! Today, we rejoice in the miracle of Easter and the profound significance of Christ's resurrection.

As we gather with family and friends to celebrate this holy occasion, may our hearts be filled with gratitude, our spirits uplifted in prayer, and our lives guided by the teachings of Christ.
May the light of His love shine brightly upon us, illuminating our path with faith, hope, and everlasting peace.
